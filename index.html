Enrollment

Choosing Philadelphia Progressive School opens the door for your child to a world of quality education, talent development, and comprehensive preparation for a successful future.

Our school is dedicated to creating an environment where every student can unlock their strengths, gain deep knowledge, and acquire the skills needed to enter the best colleges and universities. We believe that the right education is the key to success and broad opportunities for your child.

Dear parents!

If you wish to enroll your child in our school, we recommend preparing the complete set of documents well in advance.

To apply for admission to a school, you will need to provide:

your child’s birth certificate

proof of residency

medical records with up-to-date immunizations

previous academic records (if available)

completed application form submitted to the School District

It is important to submit all documents on time to ensure a smooth registration process.

Enrollment begins 2-3 months before the start of the new school year, and the number of seats in each class is limited. Student lists are formed in advance to ensure the smooth organization of the learning process. For your convenience, we provide access to an online platform where you can monitor your child’s academic progress, attendance, and communication with teachers at any time.

This way, you will always stay informed and be able to support your child at every stage of their education.
